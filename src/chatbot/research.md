# Research

## Research Areas
### Primary Focus
- Efficient object detection
- Model compression and optimization
- Representation learning
- Applied computer vision and image processing
- Internet of Things (IoT) and fog/edge computing
- Educational technology and smart campuses
- Safety and surveillance systems
- Accessibility solutions for visually impaired
- Real-time intelligent systems

## Research Orientation
Applied research with an emphasis on deployable systems. The work emphasizes translating AI from theory into systems that work reliably in real-world environments, particularly in safety, accessibility, and education. Focus on practical impact over benchmark-only results.

## Current Research Questions
- Improving detection accuracy under constrained compute
- Reducing inference latency without performance collapse
- Developing energy-efficient AI systems for edge deployment
- Building robust computer vision systems for real-world conditions
- Creating accessible and privacy-preserving AI solutions

## Research Philosophy
"My research emphasizes applied AI systems that transition from theoretical models to deployable solutions. The work targets real-world impact in safety-critical environments, educational platforms, and resource-constrained systems, balancing methodological rigor with practical applicability."
